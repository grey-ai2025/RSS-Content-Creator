## Primary Prompt — "The Historical Parallel"

The post's argument is that this has happened before. In the 1990s, "computer literacy" became a job requirement and the people who treated it as optional got left behind. AI literacy is the same moment, repeating. This image shows that moment — in the past — so the viewer immediately maps it to the present.

```
Generate a LinkedIn post image.

BRAND: Grey AI — AI training company

VISUAL CONCEPT:
A cinematic, photorealistic scene set in an early 1990s corporate office. This is a memory image — it should feel like a photograph someone took decades ago that only now makes complete sense.

The scene: A beige office bulletin board, slightly cropped, filling most of the frame. Pinned to the board is a single job posting — typed on a typewriter, slightly crooked. The posting has one line circled in red pen: "COMPUTER LITERACY REQUIRED." Around it, other pinned papers are blurred and out of focus — only this line is sharp. In the extreme background, barely visible through the depth of field, the glow of a CRT monitor on a desk.

LIGHTING: Harsh fluorescent office lighting from above, casting slightly cold, flat light. One shaft of warm light from an unseen window cuts across the bulletin board at an angle. The scene feels real, not staged.

COLOR TREATMENT: Desaturated — like a photograph that has slightly faded with age. Cool undertones. Subtle film grain throughout, heavier in the shadows. The circled text and the red pen circle remain the sharpest, most saturated element in the image. Everything else fades toward grey.

ATMOSPHERE: The image should feel like a moment from history that most viewers personally remember — the exact moment when "optional" became "required" and most people didn't notice until it was too late.

- NO generic AI imagery — no robots, no circuit boards, no glowing elements
- NO modern elements — this is purely 1990s
- The image should work without any caption: a LinkedIn professional who lived through that era recognizes the moment instantly and understands exactly what today's equivalent is
- Dark, slightly melancholic mood — the tone of looking back at a turning point you almost missed
```

## Alternate Prompt — "The Gate"

A purely visual metaphor. No historical context needed — just the feeling of standing in front of a gate that decides whether you advance. The image communicates the "promotion gate" concept through architecture and light.

```
Generate a LinkedIn post image.
Canvas: 1080x1080 pixels, square.
BRAND: Grey AI — AI training company

VISUAL CONCEPT:
A cinematic, wide-angle architectural photograph of a long modern corridor — the inside of a glass-and-steel corporate building. The floor is polished dark concrete. The walls are glass. The corridor is empty and slightly overlit by overhead strips.

At the far end of the corridor: a single glass security door, backlit. Warm amber light bleeds through the frosted glass from the other side — the light of the next floor, the next level, the next role. The door is closed. On the right side of the door frame, a small security keypad glows with a single cyan indicator light.

In the corridor, seen from behind, is a single professional figure — mid-distance, slightly out of focus. They are standing still, facing the door. Their posture communicates hesitation — they have not moved yet. They are dressed in dark corporate attire. No face visible. No identity. They could be anyone.

LIGHTING: The corridor behind the figure is lit with cool blue-white office lighting. The light through the frosted glass ahead is warm amber. The contrast between cold (where you are) and warm (where you want to be) is the emotional center of the image. The cyan keypad glow is the only saturated color accent.

COLOR TREATMENT: Predominantly desaturated — dark concrete floors, grey walls, cool overhead light. The warm amber backlight through the door and the single cyan keypad are the only warm/saturated elements. Subtle film grain throughout.

ATMOSPHERE: The image should communicate a single feeling: the gate already exists. Whether you can open it depends entirely on what you bring with you. The figure's stillness is not defeat — it is the moment just before the decision.

TEXT ON IMAGE:
None. The image communicates the concept entirely without text.

LOGO SAFE ZONE: Keep top-right corner (240x100px area, x=820 y=20) completely empty — no text, no visual elements, no gradients that obscure this zone.
WATERMARK: "Grey AI" bottom-right, 18pt, white, 60% opacity

RULES:
- Photorealistic and cinematic — architectural photography aesthetic, not a graphic
- Subtle film grain throughout — never flat or digital-clean
- The cyan keypad glow is the ONLY use of the brand accent color — it draws the eye to the mechanism of access
- NO generic AI imagery — no robots, no glowing brains, no circuit boards
- NO text on the door or walls — the concept must land without labels
- Wide depth of field: corridor recedes into the frame, the door glows at the end
- The figure should feel anonymous and universal — any professional, any industry, any career stage
- Dark, cinematic, editorial aesthetic — not corporate stock photography
```
