# Nimble Raises $47M to Give AI Agents Access to Real-Time Web Data

**Title:** Nimble Raises $47M to Give AI Agents Access to Real-Time Web Data

**Source URL:** https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/24/nimble-way-raises-47m-to-give-ai-agents-better-cleaner-data/

**Publication Date:** February 24, 2026

**Summary:**
Nimble has closed a $47 million funding round led by Norwest to build infrastructure that enables AI agents to access, validate, and structure real-time web data. The platform uses AI agents to crawl the web, verify and clean the information they find, and then organize it into queryable database tables — addressing a critical limitation in today's AI agent ecosystem, where most systems are constrained to static training data with knowledge cutoffs. Nimble's approach is designed to make real-time web information reliably accessible to downstream AI agents and workflows, filling a gap that has historically been one of the biggest practical blockers to deploying effective agentic AI in enterprise settings. The funding reflects growing investor conviction that AI agent infrastructure — not just the models themselves — is where significant enterprise value will be created.

**Key Data Points:**
- $47 million raised in new funding, with Norwest among the lead investors
- Nimble uses AI agents to search the web, validate results, clean data, and structure it into queryable tables
- The company specifically targets the gap between AI agents and real-time, current web information
- Most deployed AI models rely on training data with fixed knowledge cutoffs — Nimble aims to solve this at the infrastructure layer
- Publication date: February 24, 2026 (TechCrunch)
- The round comes amid a broader surge in AI agent infrastructure investment in early 2026

**Why It Matters:**
One of the most underappreciated bottlenecks in deploying enterprise AI agents is data freshness. AI models trained on historical data cannot reliably act on real-world conditions that change daily — market prices, regulatory updates, competitive intelligence, and more. Nimble is building the plumbing that allows agents to stay current. For enterprise architects, CTOs, and AI product teams, this represents a maturing infrastructure layer in the agentic AI stack. The $47M raise signals that investors see real-time data access as a foundational requirement for enterprise AI agents, not a nice-to-have.
