# OpenAI Raises $110 Billion in One of the Largest Private Funding Rounds in History

**Source URL:** https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/27/openai-raises-110b-in-one-of-the-largest-private-funding-rounds-in-history/

**Publication Date:** February 27, 2026

**Keyword Match:** AI-first business strategy (#12), AI infrastructure spending (related to #1 Agentic AI scaling), #AI, #GenerativeAI

## Summary

OpenAI has closed $110 billion in new private funding — one of the largest private fundraises in history — led by a $50 billion investment from Amazon, $30 billion from Nvidia, and $30 billion from SoftBank, against a $730 billion pre-money valuation. The round remains open, with additional investors expected to join. OpenAI simultaneously announced major infrastructure partnerships with both Amazon (adding $100 billion to an existing AWS compute deal, with a commitment to consume at least 2GW of AWS Trainium) and Nvidia (3GW of dedicated inference capacity plus 2GW of Vera Rubin training). ChatGPT now counts 900 million weekly active users and over 50 million consumer subscribers. The company's previous record round was $40 billion at a $300 billion valuation in March 2025 — this round more than doubles that valuation in under a year.

## Why It Matters

This is not a conventional funding story. At $110 billion, this is a bet on infrastructure at a scale that outpaces most sovereign wealth funds — more than the Apollo and Manhattan projects combined in real terms. The deals with Amazon and Nvidia are as much about compute dominance as capital: OpenAI is locking in the raw materials (chips, cloud, inference capacity) needed to run AI at population scale. The 900 million weekly user figure signals that the consumer adoption curve is not slowing. For enterprise leaders, this changes the conversation about AI vendor stability and long-term platform choices — companies partnering with OpenAI are now plugging into a system backed by the three largest players in AI infrastructure simultaneously.
