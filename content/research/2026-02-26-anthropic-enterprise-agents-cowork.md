# Anthropic Launches New Push for Enterprise Agents with Plug-ins for Finance, Engineering, and Design

**Source URL:** https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/24/anthropic-launches-new-push-for-enterprise-agents-with-plugins-for-finance-engineering-and-design/

**Publication Date:** February 24, 2026

**Keyword Match:** agentic AI, AI agents, AI in the enterprise, multi-agent systems, AI coding tools

---

## Summary

Anthropic unveiled its most aggressive enterprise push yet on Tuesday, launching a full plug-in system for its Claude Cowork platform that deploys pre-built agents for finance, legal, HR, engineering, and design departments. The announcement includes new enterprise connectors for Gmail, DocuSign, and Clay, allowing agents to pull data directly from existing corporate systems. Anthropic's head of Americas explicitly acknowledged that 2025's agent promises were "a failure of approach, not effort" and positioned this release as the delivery on those promises. Claude can now handle multi-step tasks end-to-end across Excel and PowerPoint, passing context between applications — a direct challenge to entrenched SaaS products in each vertical.

---

## Key Data Points

- Pre-built plug-ins launched at launch: finance, legal, HR, design, engineering
- New enterprise connectors: Gmail, Google Workspace, DocuSign, WordPress, Clay
- Claude Cowork available to paid Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers (currently in research preview)
- Anthropic head of Americas Kate Jensen: "2025 was meant to be the year agents transformed the enterprise, but the hype turned out to be mostly premature. It wasn't a failure of effort. It was a failure of approach."
- Matt Piccolella, Anthropic product officer: "We believe that the future of work means everybody having their own custom agent"
- Finance plug-in covers: market and competitive research, financial modeling, and data flows
- HR plug-in covers: job descriptions, onboarding materials, offer letters
- System designed with corporate IT controls: private software marketplaces, controlled data flows, customized plug-ins
- The Verge described the update as enabling Claude to handle "boring enterprise stuff" — but noted "it's where the money is"

---

## Why It Matters

Anthropic is no longer just a foundation model company — it is building the enterprise software stack that sits on top of Claude. The plug-in system directly threatens category leaders in finance software, HR platforms, and legal tech by offering Claude-powered alternatives that integrate without switching cost. For professional audiences, the signal is clear: AI agents are moving from standalone demos to embedded enterprise infrastructure. The company explicitly framing 2025 as a failed approach and 2026 as the delivery year is a high-confidence narrative for LinkedIn business audiences tracking the enterprise AI curve.
