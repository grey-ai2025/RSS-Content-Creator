## HEADLINE

- Agents operate within auditable, explainable frameworks by regulatory requirement — governance is built into the architecture, not added after
- Source credibility: Named company, confirmed executive statements, corroborated by McKinsey 2024 Global AI Survey (65% of orgs use GenAI in at least one function, most still in pilot)

## GREY AI ANGLE
This is what "from pilot to production" looks like in a regulated industry. Manulife's deployment is notable precisely because financial services governance requirements — auditability, explainability, compliance controls — are the exact constraints most enterprises use to justify staying in pilot phase. The $1B target in a compliance-constrained environment removes that argument. The question for every enterprise AI leader is no longer "can agentic AI work under governance constraints?" — it's "what is the cost of not moving?"
