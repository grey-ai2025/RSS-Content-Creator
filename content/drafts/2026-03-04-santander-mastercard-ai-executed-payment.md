## HEADLINE
An AI agent completed a live bank payment in a real production network — no human entered the final command — marking the first such transaction in Europe.

## KEY FACTS
- The payment ran on actual production banking infrastructure, not a test environment — end-to-end, no human final-step intervention
- The AI agent operated within predefined limits and permissions set by the bank and customer, in full compliance with regulatory standards
- Gartner: 33% of enterprise software applications will include agentic AI by 2028, up from under 1% today
- Source credibility: Two of Europe's largest financial institutions completing a live regulated-environment transaction — eliminates the sandbox objection

## GREY AI ANGLE
Finance has been the most cautious sector for agentic AI deployment — the objection was always regulatory compliance and auditability. A live production payment clears that objection. For any organization in a compliance-heavy sector still treating agentic AI as experimental, the most conservative adopters just moved to production.
