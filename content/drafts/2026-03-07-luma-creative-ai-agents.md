## HEADLINE
A multimodal AI agent platform is handling end-to-end creative projects — from brief to finished assets across text, image, video, and audio — and a $15 million ad campaign was reproduced in 40 hours for under $20,000.

## KEY FACTS
- $15 million, year-long ad campaign converted to localized country-specific versions in 40 hours for under $20,000 — passed the brand's internal quality controls
- Trained simultaneously on audio, video, image, language, and spatial reasoning — a single model that understands what it generates, not just generates
- Already in production at global ad agencies and brands including Adidas and Mazda
- Source credibility: Live enterprise deployments at named global agencies and brands; API available now

## GREY AI ANGLE
Creative work has been the last large professional category where AI was framed as "augmentation, not replacement." The $15M-to-$20K case study is the kind of economic comparison that forces a budget conversation, not a philosophy one. For any team managing creative production — in-house or agency — this is the moment the AI literacy question shifts from "should we experiment?" to "what do we do about the economics?"
