## HEADLINE
A Y Combinator-backed startup is replacing entire customer support teams at companies — not with software, but with an AI-native agency that takes over the full operation.

## KEY FACTS
- 6 employees, 24/7 operations, single-day integration — handles email, phone, chat, TikTok, Facebook, Telegram, and WhatsApp simultaneously
- AI currently resolves 60% of tickets autonomously; humans handle 40% — and the ratio is expected to shift further toward AI over time
- YC explicitly listed AI-native customer support agencies in its 2026 Requests for Startups — investor capital will accelerate this category
- Source credibility: $3M seed round backed by Y Combinator, General Catalyst, and founders of Dropbox, Slack, Replit, and Vercel

## GREY AI ANGLE
This is what agentic AI deployment looks like when the implementation burden is removed from the client. The "AI-native agency" model sidesteps the most common AI adoption failure — buying a tool and struggling to deploy it. For enterprise leaders, this is a preview of the organizational readiness question: not "can we use AI for this?" but "do we even need to be the ones running it?"
