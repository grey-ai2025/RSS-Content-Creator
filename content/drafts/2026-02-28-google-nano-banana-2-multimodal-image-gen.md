## HEADLINE
Google replaces its premium image generation model with a faster one that matches its quality — and makes it free to all users across every tier of its platform.

## KEY FACTS
- The new model replaces both the standard and Pro image generation variants — there is no longer a paid-only tier for image generation on the platform
- Supports up to 5 character consistency per scene and 14 distinct objects per workflow; resolution range extended from 512px to 4K widescreen
- Backed by live web knowledge, enabling real-world accuracy in rendered objects and infographics
- Source credibility: Official product launch, rolled out across Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and the consumer Gemini app simultaneously

## GREY AI ANGLE
Another professional capability just became a free commodity. Teams still paying for image generation tooling need to audit their AI stack — this is what AI tool obsolescence looks like in real time. More broadly, this is the pattern: capabilities that cost money last quarter are infrastructure this quarter.

Nano Banana 2 replaces both the standard and Pro models — not just the standard. Free to all Gemini users, including those not on paid tiers. Also deployed in Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and Flow — meaning enterprise developers get the same model as the free consumer tier.
